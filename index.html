The Biscuit Deal

The Biscuit Deal: Rules

Objective: Be the first player to reach a score of 27 points to win the game.

Game Flow: The game is played in rounds. In each round, both you and the Computer get a new set of biscuits. You each choose an action, and then the round ends.

Your Actions:

  • Eat Biscuits: The safe option. You take all the biscuits in your stash and add them to your score (1 point per biscuit). Your stash becomes zero.
  • Keep in Stash: The risky option. You keep all the biscuits in your stash. They are worth 3 points each at the end of the game, but can be stolen.
  • Steal: You try to take half of the opponent's biscuits from their stash. If their stash is empty, you get nothing.

Bluffing & Strategy:

The key to winning is understanding the bluff. When a player chooses to Keep in Stash, they are taking a risk. Use this to your advantage:

  • If you have a small stash and "Keep," you are bluffing to make the opponent think you have more.
  • If the opponent keeps a large stash, they are vulnerable! This is the perfect time for you to Steal.
